Avatar is a 2009 science fiction film directed by James Cameron. Set in the future on the alien world of Pandora, the story follows Jake Sully, a paralyzed Marine who infiltrates the Na'vi by inhabiting an alien body. The film is renowned for its groundbreaking use of 3D technology and motion capture cinematography, combined with a compelling story about colonialism, environmentalism, and the conflict between technology and nature. The franchise has grown to include multiple sequels, each expanding the world of Pandora and its rich ecosystem.
